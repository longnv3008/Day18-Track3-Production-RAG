## Tom tat hoat dong

Cong ty ABC phat hanh bao cao tai chinh nam 2024 (hop nhat).

- Doanh thu thuan nam 2024: 1.250 ty VND.
- Loi nhuan sau thue thu nhap doanh nghiep: 85 ty VND.
- Von chu so huu cuoi ky: 420 ty VND.

## Chi tieu luong chuyen

Luong tien tu hoat dong kinh doanh (nam 2024) duong 95 ty VND.
Chi tra co tuc tam ung: 12 ty VND.
